Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a hearty and flavorful dish with layers of pasta, marinara sauce, ricotta cheese, and ground beef. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook garlic until fragrant. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and parmesan cheese.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture. Repeat the layers.
  7. Top with mozzarella cheese and bake for 25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.
